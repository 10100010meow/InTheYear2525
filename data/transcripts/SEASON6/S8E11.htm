[Opening Credits. Caption: WE'RE FOLLOWING YOU, BUT NOT ON TWITTER]
[Scene: Outside a frozen lake. There is a shack on the lake. The camera begins zooming in. 1988 appears on the screen. Yancy Fry Sr walks among the trees with a stick in his left hand. A young Fry follows him with a heavy bag pack on his back. Yancy Fry Sr, who has stopped walking, turns to Fry and hits the ground with the stick.]

Yancy Fry Sr: Double time, soldier. [Yancy Fry Sr points at the shack, which has a US flag on it, with his left hand.] I want this ice-fishin' operation up and runnin' by oh-eight-hundred hours.

[Yancy Fry Sr points at the floor with his left hand. Fry expresses cold.]

Fry: It's too cold, Dad. My teen region is freezing off.

[Yancy Fry Sr, who has crossed his arms, blows a raspberry.]

Yancy Fry Sr: You dunno what cold is. [Fry reaches Yancy Fry Sr. Yancy Fry Sr uncrosses his arms, turns to his left, and resumes walking.] I once survived an entire week trapped in a Swiss glacier, eatin' nothin' but frozen Neanderthal. [Yancy Fry Sr turns to Fry.] To this day, I can't stand the taste of early hominid.

[Yancy Fry Sr turns forward. Yancy Fry Sr and Fry have reached the lake. Fry sighs.]

Fry: Let's just get inside the shack.

[Yancy Fry Sr stops walking. Fry does not.]

Yancy Fry Sr: Hold, maggot!

[Yancy Fry Sr switches the stick to his left hand and hits Fry's right leg with it.]

Fry: Ow!

[Fry steps back, stops walking, and turns to Yancy Fry Sr.]

Yancy Fry Sr: Remember the code o' the ice! Thick an' blue, tried an' true. Thin an' crispy, way too risky.

Fry: Shows what you know. [Fry turns forward and resumes walking.] Crispy doesn't even rhyme with risky. [Fry falls through the ice, screams, and resurfaces.] Help! I'm drowning!

[Fry gargles as he falls back in the water.]

Yancy Fry Sr: [calm] Don't worry, son. [Fry resurfaces.] You'll freeze before you drown.

[Fry seemingly swallows water.]
[Cut to: New New York, 3011. Several buildings have ice on top of them. We see five hovercars flying around. The camera pans down to a frozen river. Zoidberg, Leela, Professor Farnsworth, Hermes, Amy, and Fry are wearing winter clothes and walking towards the frozen river. Fry, who is holding a fishing rod with his left hand, stops walking, inhales, and exhales.]

Fry: I love ice fishing.

[Zoidberg, Leela, Bender, Hermes, Professor Farnsworth, and Amy have stopped walking. Bender is wearing a scarf.]

Professor Farnsworth: I shouldn't be out in this cold. I 'ave no protective fat, hair, or warm blood. I'm not even legally a mammal.

[Fry turns to Professor Farnsworth.]

Fry: Pfff! [Fry points at Professor Farnsworth with his right hand.] You dunno what cold is. [Fry turns away from Professor Farnsworth. Amy, Fry, Hermes, Zoidberg, Professor Farnsworth, and Leela resume walking.] I was frozen for a thousand years.

Bender: You were?! Uh. [Bender resumes walking.] Learn somethin' dumb every day.

[Bender raises his left hand.]

The box is opened again.] [off camera] Fresh-killed bait!

Amy: Can we get inside the shack? [Amy removes her hands from her waist, revealing her belly button to be showing.] I'm wearing a belly parka!

[Fry, Amy, Leela, Professor Farnsworth, Bender, Zoidberg, and Hermes enter a portable toilet with a sign reading RENT-A-POTTY - ICE FISHING SHACK nailed to it.]
[Cut to: Inside the portable toilet.]

Leela: If I understand ice fishing, and I'm pretty sure I don't, we'll need to laser-blast a hole in the ice.

[Leela pulls out a blaster with her right hand, holds it with both her hands, and points it at the floor. Fry puts his left hand on the blaster.]

Fry: No. No crazy technology. [Fry grabs a cutting tool with his left hand.] Here. Bender, use your immense Robot strength to drill a hole the old-fashioned way.

[Fry hands Bender the cutting tool. Bender takes the cutting tool with his right hand.]

Bender: I don't see any irony in that.

[Bender puts the cutting tool on the floor and begins drilling a hole, making strange sounds in the process.]

Fry: And... Woilà [sic]!

Fry: Everybody's ice-fishing! [Fry looks around.] How come nobody's ice-fishing?

[Leela, Amy, Hermes, Zoidberg, and Professor Farnsworth are gathered around Bender and are each holding a mug with both their hands.]

Bender: Hot-cocoa break.
